What we do

Icon

Mechanical

Fabricate and assemble robot parts and subsystems using machines such as mills, CNCs, band saws, and more

Icon

Control Systems

Learn to wire electrical boards and bring robots to life by writing code, from basic motor movement to advanced, fully autonomous movement

Icon

Design

Brainstorm and create robot designs using professional modeling software like Autodesk Inventor and Solidworks.

Icon

Business

Help the team build connections within the local community by recruiting, organizing events, giving presentations, and reaching out to potential sponsors.

Icon

Media

Capture and share the highlights of all our team activities by taking pictures, editing videos, posting to social media, and creating marketing materials.

Icon

Drive Team

Represent the team and operate robots during competitions and events, as a part of this highly dedicated group of members.

Join Us Today

The Flying Hedgehogs are always excited to welcome new members, regardless of skill level. We encourage anyone in grades 9-12 to explore our robotics program - no experience necessary. If you are interested in becoming a member or have any questions regarding the registration process, please contact us by email at theflyinghedgehogs@gmail.com.
