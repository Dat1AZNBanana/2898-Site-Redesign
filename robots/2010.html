FRC 2010 | Breakaway

The Game

Mimicking a soccer game, the robots from 2010’s FIRST competition “Breakaway” would launch soccer balls into goals, traverse “bumps”, and travel through tunnels in the center of the field. At the end of the match, robots raced to one of the two towers in the field and climbed for bonus points.
